I grew up loving every aspect of sports from watching them, playing them, studying them...the list goes on! All this experience at a young age leads me to where I am now, aspiring to become a professional in the sports media industry.

This website is meant to highlight both my past experiences and future aspirations of where I hope to be one day. Thank you!
